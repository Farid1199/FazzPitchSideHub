Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ "now"|date:"d F Y" }}

1. Introduction

Fazz Pitchside Hub ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: username, email address, password (hashed).
  • Profile data: position, playing level, height, preferred foot, clubs, bio.
  • Uploaded media: profile images, highlight videos, verification documents.
  • Activity data: trial applications, posts, messages, follows.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the platform experience.
  • To match players with relevant trial opportunities.
  • To enable messaging and social features.
  • To verify user identities and qualifications.
  • To improve the platform and fix issues.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your profile information is visible to other users based on your privacy settings. Verification documents are only accessible to platform administrators.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: You can view all data we hold about you in your profile.
  • Rectification: You can update your information via Edit Profile.
  • Erasure: You can delete your account via Settings > Delete Account.
  • Portability: Contact us to request a data export.
  • Restrict/Object: You can set your profile to private.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. We use localStorage for user preference settings (e.g., cookie consent, accessibility preferences).

8. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us via the Contact Page.
