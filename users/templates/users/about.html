About Fazz PitchSide Hub

Fazz PitchSide Hub is the UK's dedicated platform connecting non-league football players, clubs, scouts, and managers. We exist to bridge the gap between grassroots talent and real opportunity -- because your next chance should not depend on who you know.

Our Mission

To democratise access to football opportunities at the non-league level, ensuring that talent is discovered regardless of connections or geography. We believe every player deserves to be seen, every scout deserves reliable tools, and every club deserves access to the right talent.

Built for Every Role in Non-League Football

Players

Create a professional profile, upload highlight videos, track your stats, and discover trial opportunities at clubs across the football pyramid.

Clubs

Post trial opportunities, search for players by position and playing level, and build shortlists of promising talent for your squad.

Scouts

Discover and track players with watchlists, compare stats side by side, and verify your credentials through our tiered verification system.

Managers

Showcase your coaching qualifications, share tactical insights and match reports, and connect with clubs looking for leadership.

What the Platform Offers

AI-powered player-to-trial matching recommendations

Verified coaching and scouting qualifications

Real-time RSS news feeds from non-league clubs

Social feed with posts, follows, and direct messaging

Scout watchlists with side-by-side stat comparison

Club shortlists and trial opportunity management

Football Pathways educational hub for career development

Video uploads and YouTube highlight integration
