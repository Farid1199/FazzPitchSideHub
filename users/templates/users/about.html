About Fazz Pitchside Hub

Fazz Pitchside Hub is the UK's dedicated platform connecting non-league football players, clubs, scouts, and managers. We bridge the gap between grassroots talent and opportunity.

For Players

Create a professional profile, showcase your highlights, and discover trial opportunities at clubs across the pyramid.

For Clubs

Post trial opportunities, search for players by position and level, and build shortlists of promising talent.

For Scouts

Discover and track players with watchlists, verified credentials, and professional scouting tools.

For Managers

Showcase your coaching qualifications, share tactical insights, and connect with clubs and players.

Our Mission

To democratise access to football opportunities at the non-league level, ensuring that talent is discovered regardless of connections or geography.

Key Features

  • AI-powered player-to-trial matching recommendations
  • Verified coaching and scouting qualifications
  • Real-time RSS news aggregation from non-league clubs
  • Social features: posts, follows, messaging
  • Scout watchlists and club shortlists
  • AI-generated player bios
