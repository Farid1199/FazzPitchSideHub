Community Guidelines

Code of Conduct for Fazz PitchSide Hub

Last updated: {{ "now"|date:"d F Y" }}

Our Mission: Fazz PitchSide Hub connects grassroots football players, clubs, scouts, and managers in a professional and supportive environment. These guidelines help maintain a safe space where everyone can pursue their football career goals.

1 Respect and Professionalism

Fazz PitchSide Hub is a professional networking platform. All users must:

  • Treat every member with respect, regardless of their role, playing level, or background.
  • Use appropriate and professional language in all communications.
  • Provide constructive feedback rather than personal attacks.
  • Respect differing opinions on football tactics, training methods, and career decisions.

2 Prohibited Behaviour

The following behaviours will result in immediate action, including warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans:

Harassment and Bullying

  • Targeting, intimidating, or repeatedly contacting someone who has asked to be left alone.
  • Posting content intended to humiliate or embarrass another user.
  • Coordinating with others to harass a user (pile-ons).

Hate Speech and Discrimination

  • Content that promotes hatred or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or age.
  • Racist, sexist, homophobic, or otherwise discriminatory language.
  • Content that dehumanises individuals or groups.

Scams and Fraud

  • Fake trial opportunities or job postings.
  • Requesting money or payment from other users.
  • Phishing attempts or links to malicious websites.
  • Pyramid schemes, multi-level marketing, or fraudulent investment opportunities.

Impersonation and Fake Accounts

  • Pretending to be another person, club, or organisation.
  • Creating fake accounts to evade bans or manipulate the platform.
  • Falsely claiming qualifications, affiliations, or playing history.

Violence and Threats

  • Threatening physical harm to any individual.
  • Encouraging violence or self-harm.
  • Sharing content that glorifies violence.

Inappropriate Content

  • Sexually explicit or pornographic content.
  • Graphic violence or gore.
  • Content involving illegal activities.

Doxxing and Privacy Violations

  • Sharing personal information about others without consent (addresses, phone numbers, private photos).
  • Posting screenshots of private messages without permission.
  • Collecting or trading personal data of platform users.

3 Content Standards

When posting content on the platform:

  • Only post content you have the right to share (own photos, videos, or content with permission).
  • Be accurate and honest in your profile information and claims.
  • Do not spam or post repetitive, irrelevant content.
  • Give credit when sharing others' content where appropriate.

4 Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behaviour that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the Report button on posts, comments, or profiles.
  • Provide a clear description of the issue.
  • Include any relevant evidence (screenshots are helpful).
  • Do not engage or retaliate - let our team handle it.

Reports are reviewed confidentially. We will never reveal your identity to the reported user.

5 Consequences

Violations of these guidelines will result in the following actions, depending on severity:

Warning

First-time or minor violations. A notification will be sent explaining the issue.

Content Removal

Violating content will be removed from the platform.

Temporary Suspension

24 hours to 30 days depending on severity. You cannot post or message during this time.

Permanent Ban

Severe violations result in permanent account termination with no appeal.

6 Appeals

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error:

  • Contact us via the Contact page.
  • Include your username and details of the incident.
  • Appeals are reviewed within 7 days.
  • Decisions on permanent bans for hate speech, violence, or illegal activity are final.

7 Building a Positive Community

We encourage all members to:

  • Support fellow players, coaches, and scouts on their football journeys.
  • Share knowledge, experiences, and opportunities generously.
  • Celebrate achievements and provide encouragement.
  • Report violations to help keep our community safe.
  • Lead by example with professional, respectful behaviour.

Agreement

By using Fazz PitchSide Hub, you agree to follow these Community Guidelines. We reserve the right to update these guidelines at any time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised guidelines.
